Dataset shape: (1338, 7)

Main insights:
1. Smoker status is the strongest driver of charges:
   - Smokers: ~32,050 vs Non-smokers: ~8,434
2. Age moderately correlates with charges (corr ≈ 0.30).
3. BMI weakly correlates (corr ≈ 0.20); >52% are obese (BMI ≥ 30).
4. Number of children has very little effect (corr ≈ 0.07).
5. Mean charges differ slightly by sex and region.
6. Charges distribution is highly skewed with extreme outliers.
